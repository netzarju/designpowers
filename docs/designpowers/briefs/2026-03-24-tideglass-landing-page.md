# Design Brief: Tideglass Landing Page

## Problem Statement
Tideglass is an AI-native advisory that partners agents and humans to deliver great experiences. The landing page needs to establish credibility, communicate this positioning clearly, and convert visitors (startups through enterprise) into conversations.

## Users
- **Startup founders & operators** — evaluating whether Tideglass can help them move faster with AI. Time-poor, pattern-matching against other advisories, deciding quickly.
- **Enterprise decision-makers** — looking for trusted partners for AI transformation. Need proof of competence, may involve procurement. Possibly browsing on corporate networks with accessibility requirements.
- **Ability spectrum considerations:** visitors under cognitive load (scanning between many vendors), low-vision users, screen reader users, those on mobile in variable conditions.

## Design Direction
Bold and energetic — inspired by sites like Daybreak Ventures, Vercel, and Framer. Dark or high-contrast palette, sharp typography, confident motion. The site should feel like the future already arrived — fitting for an AI-native advisory.

## Constraints
- **Tech:** Next.js + Tailwind CSS
- **Brand:** No existing assets — logo, colours, typography, and identity to be created as part of this project
- **Accessibility:** WCAG 2.1 AA minimum
- **Scope:** Single landing page (not a multi-page site)

## Existing Design System
None — starting fresh.

## Taste Direction (Early Signal)
- Reference: Daybreak Ventures (daybreakventures.com)
- Feel: Bold, energetic, forward-moving
- Full taste calibration to follow via design-taste skill

## Success Criteria
- Visitors immediately understand what Tideglass does and who it's for
- The design feels distinctive and memorable — not another generic consultancy page
- Clear path to get in touch (contact form or email)
- Loads fast, works on mobile, meets AA accessibility

## Out of Scope
- Blog, case study pages, or multi-page navigation
- CMS integration
- Authentication or client portal
- E-commerce or pricing pages
